----------------------------------------------
Contextual substitutions (raised variants)
----------------------------------------------
(v) = any unwritten vowel
ⵏⵏ    N(v)N
ⵏⵍ    N(v)L
ⵍⵍ    L(v)L
ⵍⵏ    L(v)N
***
ⵏⵏⵏ    N(v)N(v)N
ⵏⵍⵏ    N(v)L(v)N
ⵍⵍⵍ    L(v)L(v)L
ⵍⵏⵍ    L(v)N(v)L
***
Anywhere an N or L is at the beginning of a ligature lookup, 
a raised N or L needs to be accounted for in ligature rules.
ⵏⵏⵙ    N(v)N(v)S
ⵏⵏ⵿ⵙ    N(v)[NS]
----------------
N plus ligature
----------------
ⵏⵏ⵿ⵙ    N[NS]
ⵏⵏ⵿ⴷ    N[ND]
ⵏⵏ⵿ⴸ    N[NDH]
ⵏⵏ⵿ⴼ    N[NF]
ⵏⵏ⵿ⴴ    N[NGHH]
ⵏⵏ⵿ⴶ    N[NJ]
ⵏⵏ⵿ⴾ    N[NK]
ⵏⵏ⵿ⵜ    N[NT]
ⵏⵏ⵿ⵛ    N[NSH]
ⵏⵍ⵿ⴾ    N[LK]
ⵏⵍ⵿ⵜ    N[LT]
----------------
L plus ligature
----------------
